How?

UX/UI Designers and Web Developers will trade places for one and a half hour!

  1. After introducing the Web Dev class to Wireframing, they will have 20 minutes to draw one low-fidelity screen of what they want to make for their final project;
  2. After that, they will give you their screen and you have to write the HTML for it. You'll have 40 minutes;
  3. Finally, everyone will have 20 minutes to comment: Web Developers will tell you how you could improve your code; UX/UI Designers will tell you how you can improve your wireframes!
